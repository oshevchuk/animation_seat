Sliding Seat

Benefit: Using this adjustment will allow you to change the depth of you seat so that it can be better suited to the length of your legs. Users of different heights will want to either lengthen or shorten the seat depth so that the appropriate amount of your legs will be supported.

Adjustment Details: To use this control, locate the paddle on your left hand side. Next? while holding this paddle in an "up" position, cautiously use a slight @scooting@ motion with your body (as you are seated in the chair) to make the seat's depth either increase or decrease.

Tilling

Benefit: Being able to change the tilling angle of your backrest means that you can contact that you back makes with the backrest. Set the angle adjustment at a closed angle relative to the seat in order to allow more constant contact, or at a an open angle relative to the seat in order to have the backrest make less contact with your back.

Adjustment Details: You backrest tilling angle can be adjusted with a single level as shown in the demoWith some chair models, you will need to hold up the backrest tilling angle while making your adjustment, and then release the level to lock the backrest at a specific tilting angle. While the level is unlocked, you can use your body weight to lean back and forth to change the tilling angle

Pneumatic Lift

Benefit: Using this control allows you to change the height level of your seat to better suit your individual preference as determined by your height.

Back Height Adjustment

Benefit: Using this adjustment allows you to change the height of just the backrest portion of your chair. Doing so will allow your back to receive more, or less, complete support from the backrest, and will also allow you to customize where the lumbar support area will make contact with your back.

Adjustment Details: As illustrated in this demo, adjust the height of your backrest by placing one hand on each side of your backrest either from a seated position, or while standing next to your chair. Then, while using equal pressure, employ a controlled lifting motion to raise the height of your backrest. The clicking sounds will indicate successive levels at witch you can leave the backrest height. Stop when you've reached your desired backrest height

To return the backrest to its bottommost height position, first lift the backrest all the way to the TOP, until you can lift no further. Then allow the backrest to return to its bottommost height position, being sure that the seat of your chair does not impede the backrest.

Tilling Seat

Benefit: Being able to change the tilling angle of your seat means that you can control the amount of lean backwards or forwards (see demo movie of "Forward Tilting" for detail on activation the forward lean feature) of the seat of your chair. Adjusting this tilting angle will allow you to be more comfortable in your seat by giving you control over, amongst other things, the level of pressure caused by contact between the seat surface, and the underside of your thigh/knees.

Adjustment Details: In most cases where this type of adjustment is available, the control paddle that permits this adjustment to take place can be set to an always free-floating open position, or a locked in closed position. In some cases, however, this function will be regulated by a control paddle that returns to a locked or closed position when the paddle is released. In both scenarios, to adjust the tilting angle, adjust the control paddle up (see movie for demonstration) and use your seated body weight to lean forward or backwards. Rocking Tilt

Benefit: Engaging the Rocking Tilt feature of your chair will enable you to rock back in forth as with rocking chairs (thus the name).

Adjustment Details: To activate your chairs rocking chair motion capability, lift up on the level indicated in the demo movie to permit free-floating and rocking. Please note: before using your chairs Rocking Tilt feature, please ensure that you've carefully adjusted the tension level of your chair to property counter-balance your body weight(see demo: Tension Knob).

Tension Knob

Benefit: By making this adjustment, you can make limited changes to the level of resistance you'll fell when using the Rocking Tilt feature (see demo: Rocking Tilt). Please be sure to properly adjust this resistance before utilizing the Rocking Tilt feature.

Adjustment Details: To adjust your chairs level of rocking-motion resistance, rotate the knob underneath your chairs seat counter-clockwise (from the seated users perspective) to increase resistance, or clockwise to decrease resistance. Several turns may be necessary before you'll start to notice any effect on the tension level of the chairs rocking motions. Proceed with care in making this adjustment as achieving proper tension levels is important to the safe operation of your chair

Forward Tilting

Benefit: This adjustment comes in handy when you want to lean forward as you're working, but still maintain some contact between your chairs backrest and your body. if you can imagine how children will sometimes lean forward onto the front 2 legs of a dining table chair as they study, this feature allows a very similar position. Basically, having this feature allows you to simulate a very natural human tendency and thereby improve the comfort you feel aas you will.

Adjustment Details: To tilt your chair progressively further forward (or progressively further backward), there are two adjustment controls that you will need to use.

First, activate the Rocking Tilt or Tilting Seat control (either of these two functions are activated by the same exact paddle: See demo of either of these functions by closing this info window). Move this paddle to its unlocked position so that a free-floating motion is possible. At this point, lean slightly backward in yor chair to make some weight off of the front edge of your chair
